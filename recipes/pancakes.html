pancakes

Fluffy pancakes are a breakfast staple that everyone loves. This easy recipe yields light and airy pancakes that are perfect for stacking high and drizzling with syrup.

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg

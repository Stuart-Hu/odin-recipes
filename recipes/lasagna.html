Lasagna recipe

This classic lasagna recipe is a family favorite, featuring layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. Perfect for a hearty dinner!

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 200g tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Do you have a signature recipe? One that you can bring to a party or potluck with complete confidence it'll be a winner? If not, we have one to lend you. This classic chocolate chip cookie recipe is reliable, delicious, and sure to please a crowd.

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips
